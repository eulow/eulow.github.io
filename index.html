ABOUT ME

I love dogs. I love the outdoors. I love being in the company of friends and good people. I also love to code.

Growing up, I loved breaking all the toys I had... which always got me in trouble. But being able to see all the pieces that made the toy and how all the pieces worked together to create something bigger and greater than the pieces individually, was an exhilarating feeling. It was worth the trouble.

Which is probably explains why I ended up in programming. Figuring whats parts to make and how it will it will fit with others, and then finally put it all together into a complete, functional app brings forth the same emotions as breaking my favorite toys.
